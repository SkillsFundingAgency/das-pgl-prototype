{{serviceName}} (PGL) design history

What is PGL?

Provider Growth Limit is envisaged to be a ‘hard’, adjustable and risk-based limit on the amount of apprenticeship training funding that a provider can have committed to them at any one time. This is the full context.

This documents the Alpha phase of the Provider Growth Limit project, Feb — May 2020.
